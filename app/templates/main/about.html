About James Powers

Professional Background

With over 8 years of experience in data analytics and engineering, I've had the privilege of working with some of the world's leading technology companies and consulting firms. My career has been focused on turning complex data into actionable insights and building scalable data infrastructure.

Current Role

As a Solutions Architect at Databricks, I leverage my deep experience in data engineering, analytics engineering, and ETL orchestration to help organizations build modern data platforms. I guide teams on best practices for data ingestion, modeling, orchestration, and governance.

Meta Experience (5+ Years)

During my time at Meta, I served as a Senior Analytics Engineer, where I:

  • Created infrastructure to support self-service analytics across the organization

Consulting at Deloitte

At Deloitte's Risk and Financial Advisory practice, I worked as a Senior Analytics Consultant, providing expertise across:

  • Fraud and Forensic Investigations

My work primarily focused on the Energy & Resources and Financial Services sectors.
