My Yard Design

A showcase of my Texas native plant landscape

This is my native plant landscape design, featuring drought-tolerant Texas native plants that provide year-round beauty while supporting local wildlife.

Design Philosophy

My landscape design focuses on creating a sustainable, low-maintenance garden that thrives in Central Texas conditions. By using plants native to the region, the garden requires minimal watering once established and provides critical habitat for local pollinators and wildlife.

Key Features

  • Drought-tolerant native species adapted to Texas heat
  • Pollinator-friendly blooms throughout the growing season
  • Layered planting design with trees, shrubs, perennials, and groundcovers
  • Water-wise irrigation and rainwater harvesting
  • Organic mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds

Future updates will include detailed plant lists, seasonal photos, and care logs as I expand the interactive plant database feature.
